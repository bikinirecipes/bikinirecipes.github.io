    Raspberry Smoothie

    Ingredients:

    1 c. raspberries
    1 c. almond milk (unsweetened)
    1/4 c. greek yogurt
    1/4 c. oats

    Ingredient Tips:

    Keep shelf stable almond milk on hand so you can always make healthy choices. We always buy UNSWEETENED milks. The extra sugar in your diet is not good for your health! Here are our favorite brands of unsweetened almond milk:

    JOI is the first brand on our list because the only ingredient is almonds. There are no added sugars, gums, or other ingredients! The trick to this almond milk is that it isn't a milk until after you add water and blend, which is easy to do as you're making a smoothie. If you haven't tried JOI, it is worth a taste simply due to the health benefits of skipping the unecessary ingredients found in other almond milks.
    Almond Milk Base by JOI | Make Your Own Fresh Almond Milk | Just One Ingredient | Unsweetened without Gums or Emulsifiers | Vegan, Keto, Paleo Friendly | 15 oz. | Makes up to 7 Quarts

    Kirland brand is next on our list. We are huge Costco fans because of their prices. We like this almond milk because it has a long shelf life, zero grams of added sugar, and they use organic almonds.
    Kirkland Signature Organic Almond Unsweetened Beverage, 192 fl. oz.

    Blue Diamond's Almond Breeze unsweetened vanilla blend also contains zero grams of added sugar. We like the touch of vanilla taste without the added sugar that typically comes with added flavor. It's a win-win.
    Blue Diamond Almond Breeze Unsweetened Vanilla, 32-ounce (Pack of 6)

    Last, but certainly not least is Orgain. Like some of the above, Orgain contains no added sugar, has a touch of vanilla, and is made using organic ingredients. An added bonus for this brand is that it packs 10 grams of protein into a serving (compared to about 1-3 grams per serving in the brands above).
    Orgain Organic Plant Based Protein Almond Milk, Unsweetened Vanilla - Non Dairy, Lactose Free, Vegan, Gluten Free, Soy Free, No Sugar Added, Kosher, Non-GMO, 32 Ounce (Pack of 6) (Packaging May Vary)
